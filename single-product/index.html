MAINTENACE
In order to maintain the performance of your Canon Camera and lenses, we suggest regularly checking the following:

- Checking latest firmware updates.
- Checking sensor dust regularly and if required contact us to arrange for a Check & Clean.
- Maintain your Camera body and lenses in a clean environment.
- Canon trained technicians will only use Canon Genuine Parts, using the latest tools and diagnostic equipment

You can find more information and advice in your Canon user manual.
