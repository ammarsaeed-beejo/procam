About us

Procam Electronics is a leading company in supplying high quality Imaging and Video products, Providing ideal solutions in digital imaging technologies for Governmental entities, NGOs, corporates and consumers. With more than 15 year of experience in the Jordanian Market, we have provided our clients with multiple solutions from image capturing to high-quality printout, expanding to home and office supplies Serving our clients with more than 20 well-known brands in the latest products for Cinematography, Professional Video & Photography industry. Procam Electronics is an official distributor of Canon Products and the authorized service center of Canon Cameras & Lenses in Jordan.

MAINTENACE
In order to maintain the performance of your Canon Camera and lenses, we suggest regularly checking the following:

- Checking latest firmware updates.
- Checking sensor dust regularly and if required contact us to arrange for a Check & amp; Clean.
- Maintain your Camera body and lenses in a clean environment.
- Canon trained technicians will only use Canon Genuine Parts, using the latest tools and diagnostic equipment

You can find more information and advice in your Canon user manual.
